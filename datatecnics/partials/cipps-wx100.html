CIPPS™ WX100

CIPPS™ WX100 is the world's first system that through the use of intelligent smart materials and innovative nano sensors and electronics can directly monitor the condition of pipelines on a real-time basis. To put it succinctly, it can make pipelines into intelligent self-thinking and self-analyzing systems fit for purpose that benefit users throughout the value chain.

CIPPS™ WX100 is underpinned by the same core technology as the CIPPS™ EX100. CIPPS™ WX100 will focus on measuring water pipeline stress levels at a molecular level, forecasting micro-fractures and using software algorithms to constantly monitor the integrity and resiliency of the pipeline throughout its network.

It will be able to notify operators immediately of bursts and leaks, enabling rapid reactive maintenance. It will also offer real-time monitoring and predictive ability so that targeted, cost-effective, preventative maintenance can be quickly carried out, preventing unnecessary downtime or loss of water supply across any region protected by CIPPS™ WX100.

Capabilities

CIPPS™ WX100 is a completely novel, industry-disruptive product with no true competitors-offering a completely unique proposition.

  1. CIPPS™ WX100 removes the human element and notifies operators immediately in the event of a breach.
  2. CIPPS™ WX100 offers predictive ability so the pipe can be upgraded even before there is a breach.
  3. Operators are fully aware of the level of leaks within system so can budget for maintenance accordingly.
  4. CIPPS™ WX100 is fully integrated into pipelines, offering complete protection.
  5. CIPPS™ WX100 reduces labor requirements by using integrated automated software and nanotechnology.

Operations

IIOT is central to CIPPS™ WX100. Tens of thousands of sensors sheath and encase our water pipelines delivering real-time, instantaneous information to operators no matter their geolocation. Instead of being forced to rely on jury-rigged solutions, water operators using our sophisticated software interface know exactly the location of the leak and can react accordingly.

Using very low power with autonomous communication, Datatecnics nano-sensors simultaneously provide operators with both rich real-time information and prioritize data for the needs of the end user.

With many regions losing up to 50% of their water just in distribution, CIPPS™ WX100 has true potential for any prospective operator seeking to provide abundant water supplies to their end users.

Industrial And Commercial Applications

CIPPS™ WX100 has been specifically designed for use by water operators. It will be designed to fit on both new pipes, be retro-fitted cost-effectively wherever needed, and be fully functional in both the coldest and hottest environments possible.
